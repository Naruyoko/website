Extensoins to Arrow Notations

Hello. Today I will try to make an extension to the Uparrow Notations. Uparrow Notaitons is defined as following:

  • \(a\uparrow b=a^b\)
  • \(a\uparrow^n b=a\uparrow^{n-1}a\uparrow^{n-1}a...\uparrow^{n-1}a\) (b copies of a) for \(n\gtq2\)
And \(\uparrow^n\) means \(\uparrow\uparrow...\uparrow\) (n \(\uparrows\)).
Now, I interpreted the multiple \(\uparrow\)s as multiplication, so first I thought of multiplication by a number. By the generic rule of hyper operations, I thought of the following:
\(a\left(\uparrow^nm\right)b=a\uparrow^{n}a\uparrow^{n}a...\uparrow^{n}b\) (m copies of a)
Which makes little more sense if it was interpreted like that:
\(a\left(\uparrow^nm\right)b=f^m(b)\) where \(f(x)=a\uparrow^nx\)
So, the generic rule can be rewritten like this:
\(a\uparrow^n b=a\left(\uparrow^{n-1}(b-1)\right)a=a\left(\uparrow^{n-1}b\right)1\) Then, as I do this, I thought of making more complex expression can be done with this.
I saw that the multiplicated arrow rule goes following:
\(a\left(\uparrow^nm\right)b=a\left(\underset{m}{\underbrace{\uparrow^n+\uparrow^n+...+\uparrow^n}}\right)b=a\uparrow^na\uparrow^n...\uparrow^nb\) So, I thought I could have each added uparrows to have different number of arrows. This lead to the following:
\(a\left(\uparrow^{n_1}+\uparrow^{n_2}+...+\uparrow^{n_k}\right)b=a\uparrow^{n_1}a\uparrow^{n_2}...\uparrow^{n_k}b\)
This notations are not communitive, so it should not be calculated inside the arrows.
Here, I defined the arrow multiplication with paranthesis.
\(a\left(\uparrow^{n_{1,1}}+\uparrow^{n_{1,2}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{1,k}}\right)\left(\uparrow^{n_{2,1}}+\uparrow^{n_{2,2}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{2,l}}\right)b \newline=f^{\left(\uparrow^{n_{2,1}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{2,l}}\right)}(b)\;\textup{where}\;f(x)=a\left(\uparrow^{n_{1,1}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{1,k}}\right)x=a\uparrow^{n_{1,1}}...\uparrow^{n_{1,k}}x \newline=\underset{a\left(\uparrow^{n_{2,1}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{2,l}}\right)b}{\underbrace{a\left(\uparrow^{n_{1,1}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{1,k}}\right)...\left(\uparrow^{n_{1,1}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{1,k}}\right)b}}\)
Addition of paranthesis can be just connected.
\(a\left(\left(\uparrow^{n_{1,1}}+\uparrow^{n_{1,2}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{1,k}}\right)+\left(\uparrow^{n_{2,1}}+\uparrow^{n_{2,2}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{2,l}}\right)\right)b \newline=a\left(\uparrow^{n_{1,1}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{1,k}}\right)\left(a\left(\uparrow^{n_{2,1}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{2,l}}\right)b\right) \newline=a\left(\uparrow^{n_{1,1}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{1,k}}+\uparrow^{n_{2,1}}+...+\uparrow^{n_{2,l}}\right)b\)
